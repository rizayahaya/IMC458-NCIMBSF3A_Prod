I give what I can—not in money, but in time, effort, and commitment to my community.

I began my volunteer journey in 2010 as part of the event crew for Youth’10, which opened opportunities for me to support major sports and international events such as SCKLM, OCBC Cycle, MotoGP, LIMA and Defence Services Asia. Beyond event management, I was an active member of Volunteer KL, contributing to community programs including feeding the homeless and preparing bubur lambuk for the less fortunate and night-shift workers during Ramadan. I also have participated in "Jelajah Sukarelawan Perdana", organized by Pertubuhan Belia Perdana and this is an initiative to assist flood victims in 2017 and was actively involved in youth-driven programs such as Rakan Belia and IM4U under the Ministry of Youth and Sports. Through these experiences, I developed strong teamwork, leadership, and community engagement skills while contributing to social impact at both local and national levels.
